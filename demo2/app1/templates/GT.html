Gujarat Titans

Indian Premier League (IPL) Team

About Team

Gujarat Titans is a popular IPL franchise team representing Gujarat. The team won the IPL title in its debut season in 2022.

Team Information

  • Founded: 2022
  • Home Ground: Narendra Modi Stadium, Ahmedabad
  • Captain: Shubman Gill
  • Coach: Ashish Nehra
  • IPL Titles: 1

Famous Players
